    What is DevOps?
    • DevOps is the combination of cultural philosophies and practices used in cooperation that emphasizes software developers (Devs) and IT maintenance operations (Ops).
    • Automated software delivery and architectural updating procedures are used to make the building, testing, and distribution software more agile, frequent, and reliable.

    DevOps use method:
    • Miniaturization of corporate organization uses the optimally effective team size (8–10) to undertake company procedures and API-related concepts are used to interconnect and interface teams.
    • Employees are encouraged to engage in projects they are interested in, to emphasize responsibility.
    • Continually disseminates and implements DevOps cultural philosophy to streamline software development and maintain competitiveness.
    • With the DevOps models, development and operations teams are no longer "fighting alone". Sometimes, the two teams will be combined into a single team and the engineers will be in charge of the entire application lifecycle, including development and testing, deployment and operations. Capabilities are developed that go beyond just a single function.

    The strengths of DevOps:
    • Speed
      High-speed mobility allows customers to innovate more quickly, adapt closer to changing markets, and grow more effectively while driving business. The DevOps model can help your developers and operations teams to attain these results. For example, miniaturized services and continual delivery gives the team ownership over services, and can then complete version updates quickly.

      • ➜ According to our experience, hours can be shortened to minutes.

    • Quick delivery
      Increases the rate and speed of version releases, allowing you to innovate and improve your products even faster. You can release functions and error correction even faster, allowing even faster responses to customer needs and building competitive advantages. Continual integration and delivery are both practices output by automated software (from establishment to deployment).

      • ➜ According to our experience, error rate can be completely avoided.

    • Reliable
      Ensures quality of application updates and changes to infrastructure, meaning that you can reliably deliver at faster speeds while maintaining a positive end-user experience. Uses practices such as continuous integration and delivery to test each change for practicality and security. Monitoring and logging of practices can help you understand efficacy in real time.

    • Expansion
      Large-scale operations and management of your infrastructure and development procedure. Since it is automated and consistent, it can help you manage complicated or fluctuating systems more effectively while also lowering risk. For example: infrastructure as code uses a repeatable and more effective method to manage your development, testing, and production environment.
      • ➜ We can help you use IaaS to quickly deploy resources across multiple geographical regions with enhanced reliability.

    • Security
      Act quickly, all while retaining control and maintaining compliance. The DevOps model can be used without sacrificing security, due to automated compliance policy, fine adjustments, and configuration management technology. For example: compliance can be defined and tracked at larger scales by use of infrastructure as code and policy as code.
