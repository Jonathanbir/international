    Introduction

    With a global network of 27 Chunghwa Telecom's Smart Healthcare solution delivers a powerful ecosystem of intelligent, connected healthcare services. By integrating private 5G networks, medical IoT devices (IoMT), cloud computing, and telemedicine, we help hospitals and clinics achieve safer, more efficient, and patient-centered care. Whether you're building a smart hospital, enabling remote care, or upgrading to automated wards, our platform supports your digital health transformation end-to-end.

    Features

    Smart Hospital & Ward Automation

    Real-time dashboards, smart IV drips, automated dispensing cabinet, and EPDs improve workflow efficiency and reduce human error.

    Telemedicine Platform (SynDr)

    Seamlessly conduct remote consultations with multi-source video, patient record sharing, secure cloud storage, and collaboration tools for physicians.

    Medical IoT Devices for Real-Time Monitoring

    Monitor vital signs such as heart rate, oxygen, blood pressure, temperature, and glucose remotely or onsite via certified 5-in-1 health monitoring kits.

    5G-Enabled ICT Infrastructure

    Robust and scalable multi-network design (Wi-Fi, 4G/5G, Ethernet) ensures low latency and uninterrupted healthcare services, both in- and out-of-hospital.

    Strengths

    End-to-End Integration

    Our solution connects networks, IoMT, cloud, and hospital systems through Chunghwa Telecom’s robust infrastructure.

    Enterprise-Grade Security

    Secured with AES+RSA encryption, HL7 FHIR compliant, and certified for data privacy and interoperability in Taiwan.

    Global and Scalable Network Infrastructure

    With private 5G and fiber, we support both local and cross-border telehealth, ensuring your network is scalable and future-ready.

    Proven Track Record

    Trusted by leading hospitals with over ten successful deployments in Taiwan and Southeast Asia.
