    GoodLinker | AIoT applications

    GoodLinker is a startup focused on AIoT technologies. It aims to bring the benefits of smart manufacturing to more factories. Cloud computing technology is used to produce a reliable, friendly, and interactive system. It can quickly visualize the data from production line equipment, giving the company the ability to monitor the status of its factory anywhere, anytime.

    What are the challenges?
    • GoodLinker helps SMEs import smart equipment monitoring systems and implementing digital transformation. But it had not yet planned for a multifunctional cloud management system.
    • GoodLinker is a young group that was established in 2018. With their spirit of entrepreneurship, innovation, and creation, the team continually creates new ideas and turns these innovative ideas into reality. It is engaged in creating interactive systems that link equipment and users by use of edge computing.
    • Such a system requires expandable modularized hardware and a large and dynamic cloud space, eliminating the need for customized development and a self-built server room while also greatly reducing build costs. It can be adjusted as needed and is suitable for factory owners, the second-generation of owners, or a traditional industry looking to undergo transformation.
    Chunghwa Telecom's solution:
    • Chunghwa Telecom's cloud management team was available for support 24-7. It offered professional technology consulting, system efficiency monitoring, automated power on/off, high availability architectural design, high security and recommendations on compliance.
    • We used AWS Trusted Advisor to inspect the AWS cloud environment, including architecture, costs, effectiveness, and Security, and then produce recommendations for optimization.
    • Amazon CloudWatch collects usage rates and operational status for the network, computation, and storage; the results of which are then given professional recommendations to ensure the security of key services.
    • Used Chunghwa Telecom's proprietary cloud management platform with its functionality for self-backup and regular power on/off protects information from loss while saving costs for the customer.
    How were the obstacles overcome?
    • Chunghwa Telecom's cloud management team, composed of multiple AWS professionally certified architects and Security experts, provided GoodLinker with professional consulting and recommendations, while also alleviating its concerns over architectural design and Security in real time.
    • With AWS's reliable, convenient, diverse cloud services, Chunghwa Telecom was able to implement its solution at the customer end.
    Results of cooperation:
    • Chunghwa Telecom's cloud management team helped GoodLinker cut 60% of human resource costs and comprehensively monitored the management process of the AWS system.
    • Chunghwa Telecom's cloud management team was available for support 24-7. It offered professional technology consulting, system efficiency monitoring, automated power on/off, high availability architectural design, high security and recommendations on compliance.

    AWS Identity and Access Management、AWS EC2、AWS Cognito、AWS IoT Core、Amazon DynamoDB‎、AWS SNS、AWS Lambda、Amazon S3、AWS CloudWatch、AWS CloudTrail、AWS Config、AWS System Manager、Amazon CloudFront、AWS Route 53。
